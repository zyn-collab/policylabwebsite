Housing · analysis
Housing connects everything
Housing scarcity in the Maldives functions as an upstream cause of problems that policymakers treat as separate crises – fertility decline, disease transmission, educational gaps, family breakdown, and youth crime all trace back to the physical conditions in which people live.
