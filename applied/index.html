AI prompting tool that generates expert-level prompts for government document types – cabinet papers, SOPs, research designs, UN formats. Detailed prompts often run to a full page of specific instructions. Customized for each office's formats, style guides, and reference documents.

Policy analysis
Policy Builder
An interactive policy analysis tool that builds on the UNESCAP SP2LNOB framework and extends it to include non-cash interventions, making it a more comprehensive tool for designing social protection responses. Guides users through targeting and eligibility, benefit design, implementation planning, staffing, and capacity requirements – then generates a structured policy analysis document. Planned for wider rollout once funding mechanisms are secured.
