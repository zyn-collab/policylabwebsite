Consulting services

Policy expertise for any client

Deeply researched insights, polished publications, and guided expertise for governments, NGOs, international organisations, and the private sector. Our consulting engagements sustain the broader policy mission – every project funds the research and community work we do alongside it.

What we offer

Services

Policy Research & Analysis

In-depth, evidence-based research and analysis on policy questions, delivered as briefs, full reports, or background papers.

Data Analysis & Reports

Quantitative and qualitative data analysis, survey design, statistical modelling, and polished professionally designed publications and technical documents.

Monitoring & Evaluation

Design and implementation of M&E frameworks, data collection systems, and performance tracking tools at organisational or programme level.

AI Integration Consulting

Integrating AI tools into organisational workflows – from document generation and policy drafting to data processing and institutional knowledge management.

Workshops & Seminars

Practical trainings on policy implementation, M&E, planning and foresight methodologies, AI integration, data collection and usability, and more.

Policy Briefings for Legislators

Concise, evidence-based briefings on specific policy areas for policymakers, legislators, and decision-makers who need to get up to speed quickly.

Custom Data Tools

Interactive data products, visualizations, dashboards, knowledge management systems, and policy applications built for your specific needs.

Stakeholder Engagement

Focus groups, consultations, validation sessions, and participatory processes designed to gather evidence and build consensus.

Support for International Firms

Political and policy environment briefings, market guidance, and regulatory navigation for international firms seeking to invest, expand, or bid in the Maldives – including legal and tax briefings through our partner firms.
