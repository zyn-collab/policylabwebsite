economic diversification

The Maldives faces several compounding structural vulnerabilities: tourism dependence concentrating GDP risk in a single sector, reliance on imports for nearly everything from food to fuel to construction materials, and geographic dispersion across 187 inhabited islands that blocks the economies of scale available to most countries. These briefs map specific diversification strategies with different time horizons and implementation requirements, alongside the operational tools and legal instruments needed to act on them.

Economic diversification and decentralization
The structural case for diversification beyond tourism: import dependence, foreign currency concentration, geographic dispersion limiting economies of scale, and the fiscal vulnerability of building a national economy on a single seasonal sector. Maps the interlocking strategies and the sequencing between them.

Northern development · transport
Hanimaadhoo airport as a low-cost carrier hub
Developing Hanimaadhoo as a budget airline gateway to India's middle-class travel market. Creates economic opportunity for the northern region and brings foreign currency inflows without cannibalising the luxury resort segment that uses Velana.

Visa programme · forex strategy
Remote work visa programme
Attracting high-earning global remote workers to live and spend in the Maldives. Generates forex inflows dispersed across island economies rather than concentrated in resorts, while building international professional networks and diversifying the local spending base.

Decentralisation · employment
Remote office spaces in islands
Professional shared office infrastructure on islands enabling island residents to access white-collar employment without migrating to Male'. Satellite wings of Male' companies operating in atoll centres, with the SOP and license agreement to implement them.

Manufacturing · resilience
3D printing and CNC mini-factories
Distributed digital fabrication reducing dependence on imported spare parts and creating local manufacturing capability. Small-scale production hubs that can print replacement parts, custom components, and low-volume products without waiting for international shipping.
